## Line-by-line diff against v1

| Line | v1 | v2 | Type | Reason |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 3 | 口の中に**出す** | 口の中に**出る** | Grammar | In 言葉が…出す, an intransitive-style subject is paired with a transitive verb — ungrammatical. Changed to the intransitive 出る; mora unchanged (da-su→de-ru, both 2). |
| 5 | **怖った** | **怖くて** | Grammar | 怖い is an adjective; there is no verb 怖る, so こわった is invalid. 怖くて (4 mora) is the same length as こわった (ko-wa-t-ta, 4 mora). **Finalized: 怖くて (user confirmed 2026-07-03; the alternative 恐れた is discarded).** |
| 11 | **チャーク** | **チョーク** | Word choice | Standard loanword spelling (chalk→チョーク). Mora unchanged (both 3). |
| 11 | 靜 | 静 | Glyph form | Old glyph form → new glyph form, easier for subtitles/SynthV; reading and count unchanged. |
| 16 | **逃げわず** | **逃げずに** | Grammar | 逃げわず is invalid (ichidan verbs have no わ sound shift). Replaced with the equal-length 逃げずに (4 mora). |
| 23 | **歩い続ながら** | **歩き続けながら** | Grammar | The original is missing kana and is invalid. The correct form is 9 mora, 1 over budget; note-split plan below. Mora-preserving alternative: **生き続けながら** (i-ki-tsu-zu-ke-na-ga-ra, 8) "even through hardships, keep on living" — a semantic shift, but grammatical. |
| All | v1 romaji | Regenerated | Romaji | Fixes: u ga bu→u ka bu (line 14 浮かぶ), me gu ru→me ku ru (line 13 めくる), cyaa ku→cho o ku (line 11); line 12 は uniformly written wa. Lines 21/22/24 had no romaji attached in the original, filled in from the identical lines. |

## Line 23 note-split plan (**finalized: 歩き続けながら + note split**, user confirmed 2026-07-03; alternative 生き続けながら discarded)

In v1 this phrase is sung on 8 notes as あ-る-い-つ-づ-な-が-ら; v2 needs 9 (あ-る-き-つ-づ-**け**-な-が-ら),
with the new け falling between づ and な. Plan: listen to this phrase in `refs/anon_version.mp3`,
find whichever of づ or な has the longer duration, split it in half, and give the latter (or former) half to け.
Empirical guess: the な of ながら usually runs long mid-phrase, so splitting な is the first choice.
**This step requires you to listen.** Criterion: after splitting, sing along once — け must not steal the beat and な must not feel rushed.
If both notes are too short, switch to the alternative 生き続けながら — exactly 13 mora, no split needed.
